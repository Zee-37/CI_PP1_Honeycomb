Every bee needs a hive...

As you approach the doors of Honeycomb the busy Beehive Street, you're immediately greeted by a neon sign that reads "Coffee Wine Cocktail". Like an oasis in a dessert, Our Cafe is located in a busy commercial area and serves as a reminder to take a break.
Since 2020, our little cafe has grown in size, reputation and community. We only use the finest of coffee beans to guarantee all your coffee needs are satisfied. Don't believe us? Here from our hive!

Meet the owners...

Tish (left) and Zainab (right) are partners in all sense of the word. Tish, previously working in investment banking, had an interest in mixology and was always seen behind the bar at parties and social events.
Zainab's coffee addiction led her to swear off buying coffee and brewing her own cup everyday. Baking regularly is a family tradition for Zainab, which was why, during the pandemic, the two initially decided on starting a bakery. One thing led to another and Honeycomb was born.
Zainab and Tish operate on principals of 'family, flavours and fun' and wish their business will serve as a safe space for their customers, regardless of our differences.
