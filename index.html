Coffee - Cocktails - Community

Honeycomb is a cafe and cocktail bar in one. Enjoy our decadent baked goods and speciality coffee during the day, you will be enticed to return for a cocktail and small bites in the evening. Located in the middle of the ever buzzing beehive street, this unique space is a true oasis and a must-visit for any coffee and cocktail lover!

Introducing Cocktails!!

You asked and we listened! Thanks to your continuous support, we are super excited to introduce cocktails to our menu, available after 6pm or anytime for Sunday brunch. Whethere you're craving an espresson or a green tea, espresson martini or a purple rain, come on down to Honeycomb and enjoy two for one cocktails until the end of February!

Venue for hire

Do you have an event you want to host but not sure where to host it? We host book clubs, small gatherings, birthday, games nights, karaoke, drag shows, workshops, you name it! Choose between the vibrant atmosphere of the cafe or the more intimate setting of the garden.
As part of our plan to give back to the community, local charities can book our venue for free. Contact us for booking enquiries.
